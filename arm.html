ARM One - Unnified Wealth

ARM One is a unified wealth management platform. It is designed for both personal and corporate investors by consolidating disparate wealth services into one intuitive experience.

The ARM One application is the central hub for accessing and managing the full spectrum of ARM financial products including investments, pensions, mutual funds, stocks, and trustees.

The Challenge

Despite ARM’s strong market presence, users faced a fragmented digital experience across multiple financial products.

Issues such as disjointed access to multiple investment portfolios, unclear performance visibility (especially around net asset values), and a lack of unified client profile to streamline onboarding and personalization.

These challenges diluted user trust and increased the burden on customer service teams. ARM One app needed a modern platform that delivered clarity, cohesion, and confidence — without sacrificing the sophistication expected of a premium investment partner.

Project Goals

The mission was to build an intelligent, transparent, user-centered platform that:

- Unified all ARM products under a single digital ecosystem
- Simplified financial actions - subscription, monitoring, liquidation
- Provided complete asset transparency, including real-time performance trends
- Delivered personalized investment guidance via profiling and risk assessment
- Enabled management collaboration through in-app financial advisor engagement
- Created a consistent omnichannel experience across web and mobile

The result needed to satisfy first-time investors and seasoned corporate clients alike.

Anchoring the Experience in Transparency

User testing revealed a strong desire for clear visibility into net asset values, contribution history, and performance over time. This insight guided:

- A performance-first visual hierarchy
- Rich contextual charts for asset growth
- Clear gain/loss indicators
- Breakdown views for each product category

The interface brings financial clarity to the forefront - eliminating ambiguity.

Client Profiling & Personalized Guidance

A refined onboarding flow uses client data to construct a dynamic profile, enabling:

- Risk tolerance assessment
- Product suitability suggestions
- Transaction limits and compliance alignment
- Relevant recommendations from the financial manager

This transforms ARM One from a transactional tool into a personal wealth companion.

Risk Profile Assessment

To support responsible investment decisions, the platform evaluates:

- Investment goals
- Time horizons
- Liquidity preferences
- Appetite for market volatility

User-friendly questionnaires and guided prompts ensure accessibility for beginners while giving experts the specificity they expect.

Clear Call-to-Actions & Accessible Navigation

With high-value transactions at stake, clarity and trust were non-negotiable. We implemented:

- Bold and consistent CTAs for high-intent actions
- Contextual help tips for every sensitive entry point
- Accessible typography and color contrast
- Predictable navigation patterns across devices

This ensured that users - from executives to first-time savers - could operate the platform confidently.

Design Methodology

- Discovery & Research
- Stakeholder interviews
- User journey mapping
- Competitor analysis
- Behavioural studies across investor categories

UX Architecture
- Information architecture unifying all product types
- User flows for subscription, oversight, and withdrawal
- Experience maps for both individual and corporate users

UI & Interaction Design Modular dashboards Multi-product portfolio views Dynamic charts and insights Accessible color palette aligned with ARM brand equity

Validation: - Usability testing sessions - A/B testing on performance visualization - Iterative refinements to navigation, CTAs, and grouping logic - The design process remained deeply tied to user evidence — not assumptions.

Conclusion

ARM One stands as a modern, intelligent, and unified investment experience — empowering users with transparency and unlocking new revenue opportunities for ARM. By balancing human-centered design, robust financial logic, and omnichannel accessibility, the platform redefines how individuals and institutions engage with long-term wealth creation.

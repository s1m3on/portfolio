Meritrade - NGX Trading Platform

Meritrade is a digital trading platform designed around the Nigerian Stock Exchange (NGX), enabling users to buy and sell stocks, commodities, and bonds through a streamlined mobile experience. The platform integrates a secure digital wallet for funding and settlement, a comprehensive portfolio dashboard for monitoring investments, and market analysis tools that surface actionable insights. Built on Meristem's long-standing credibility in financial services, Meritrade modernizes stock trading while preserving the trust expected from a well-established institution.

The Challenge

While Meristem had strong brand recognition and investor trust, its digital trading experience faced several usability and engagement challenges:

- High friction onboarding, slowing time to first trade
- Complex trading interfaces that overwhelmed non-professional investors
- Limited personalization, requiring users to manually track market movements
- Underutilized portfolio insights, reducing long-term engagement
- Accessibility gaps for an older demographic

The platform needed to appeal to a broad audience -- from experienced traders to first-time investors -- without compromising clarity, confidence, or performance.

Project Goals

The objective was to design a trading platform that:

- Simplified access to NGX trading for all experience levels
- Reduced onboarding time and accelerated first engagement
- Enabled informed decision-making through market insights and analysis
- Built trust through clarity, tone, and visual consistency
- Increased trading frequency via timely, personalized engagement
- Delivered an accessible experience for users aged 35-65

The product needed to feel professional, dependable, and intuitive -- not intimidating.

UX Strategy & Design Rationale

1. Designing for Trust and Accessibility
With a core demographic of users aged 35-65, accessibility was foundational to every design decision. We focused on:

- Clean layouts with clear separation of content
- Refined, legible typography
- High-contrast color usage
- Predictable navigation patterns

These choices reduced cognitive load while reinforcing Meristem's credibility.

2. Streamlined Onboarding for Faster Activation
User research revealed that lengthy onboarding flows were a primary drop-off point. To address this, we:

- Simplified identity verification steps
- Introduced progressive disclosure
- Provided contextual guidance during setup
- Reduced the path to first trade

The result was a significantly faster time to first meaningful action.

3. Portfolio Dashboard as a Central Command Center
The portfolio view was redesigned to surface what matters most:

- Current holdings
- Asset allocation
- Real-time performance
- Gains and losses over time

By prioritizing clarity over complexity, users could quickly understand their financial position without navigating multiple screens.

4. Market Trend Analysis for Informed Trading
To support confident decision-making, Meritrade integrates market trend tools that:

- Highlight price movements
- Surface volume changes
- Provide contextual market signals

These insights are presented in a simplified, visual format -- designed to inform, not overwhelm.

5. Personalized Trade Recommendations & Alerts
User research identified a strong demand for proactive engagement. We introduced:

- Personalized trade suggestions based on user behavior
- User-defined push notifications for price changes, market movements, and reminders
- Alerts aligned with individual portfolio interests

This feature bridged the gap between passive monitoring and active trading.

6. Integrated Digital Wallet for Seamless Transactions
Meritrade's built-in wallet enables users to:

- Fund accounts securely
- Execute trades without friction
- Track transaction history transparently

By eliminating external dependencies, the platform creates a cohesive trading flow from funding to execution.

Design Methodology

Discovery & Research
- User interviews across multiple age groups
- Behavioral analysis of trading patterns
- Competitive benchmarking against local and global trading platforms

Experience Architecture
- Simplified information hierarchy
- Clear separation between trading, portfolio, and analysis
- Reduced steps across critical flows

Interface Design
- Brand-aligned visual language
- Accessible typography and spacing
- Consistent tone of voice to reinforce trust

Validation
- Usability testing on onboarding and trade execution
- Iterative refinement of alerts and dashboards
- Feedback-driven UI improvements

Design decisions were consistently validated against real user behavior.

Solution

Meritrade delivers a refined trading experience through:

- Intuitive NGX stock, bond, and commodity trading
- Secure digital wallet integration
- Clear, actionable portfolio dashboards
- Simplified onboarding and account setup
- Personalized trade recommendations and push notifications
- Market trend analysis tailored to user interests

The platform balances professional-grade capability with everyday usability.

Impact

User Impact
- Faster onboarding and reduced friction
- Improved clarity around investment performance
- Increased confidence in trading decisions
- More consistent engagement through timely alerts

Business Impact
- Increased trading activity driven by personalized notifications
- Stronger user retention across age demographics
- Reinforced digital trust in the Meristem brand
- Higher platform adoption among new investors

Meritrade successfully bridges traditional financial credibility with modern digital trading expectations.

Conclusion

Meritrade demonstrates how thoughtful UX design can transform stock trading from a complex task into a confident, accessible experience. By aligning user needs, brand trust, and market realities, the platform empowers investors to engage more actively -- while strengthening Meristem's position in Nigeria's digital trading ecosystem.
